Who are we

Young Happy Healthy and Safe is a registered non- profit making organization operating in Eastern Province of Zambia for over 15 years, and currently operating in Chipata, Chipangali, Kasenengwa, Katete, Nyimba and Chadiza districts. The organization has implemented different projects such as: Community Care and Support funded by the Stephen Lewis Foundation, Happy Health and Safe (HHS) a Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) project funded by funded by International HIV/AIDS Alliance, Ministry of Health/Global Fund, and Southern African Aids Trust, ( SAT Zambia), Safe Mother- hood Project funded by Zambia Led- Preventive Initiative (ZPI –USAID), Care and Support to OVCs supported by PEPFAR small grants, Emerging Voices Project (EVP) supporting female sex workers funded by SIDA and SRHR – HIV Knows No Borders Project funded by Kingdom of Netherlands, Community Health Systems Strengthening (SBH-USAID). YHHS has extensive experience working with government departments at national, district and community level, displaying effective collaboration with other NGOs and community structures. YHHS has largely specialized in the implementation of Sexual Reproductive Health and Rights, Comprehensive Sexuality and Life Skills Education, menstrual hygiene programme in schools and HIV prevention interventions among the most at risk groups such as Adolescents and Young People, sex workers and pregnant women.

Economic Empowerment

YHHS is providing a systematic approach such as working with neighborhood health committees and health centre committees, traditional, cultural and religious leaders for understanding the essential components of community systems and for the design, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of community – based interventions to strengthen these components. Community System Strengthening has a strong focus on capacity building on human and financial resources (training of community change agents in health, education and village Banking Savings Model). Enabling community actors such as Chiefs, headmen, Indunas, teachers, Parents, men and women to play a full and effective role alongside health and social welfare systems for health and safe communities. Children protection Young Happy Healthy & Safe (YHHS) through its Child Safeguarding Policy document in place believes that children are human beings and have rights. YHHS ensure that children go to school as well as learning in a good and safe place, eating well, protecting children from all forms of violence and danger, getting well quickly when the child is unwell, and preventing children from suffering all because they were born in a poor family. The organization ensures that children have the right to participation and protection among other rights and are consulted in decision-making processes concerning their own healthcare, consistent with their evolving capacities. The YHHS organization endeavor to protect children from any form of injustice and harm including child labour, early of forced marriages, gender-based violence and child sexual abuse.
